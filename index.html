Research

My research focuses on the development of continual learning language agents that can adapt and generalize across tasks and environments without the need for extensive retraining.

I've worked on projects involving:

  • Developing autonomous agents using frozen language models.
  • Enhancing performance through dynamic memory and causal abstractions.
  • Exploring rapid task adaptation in text-based environments.

Projects

Discover my key projects:

  • IISc Project: Developed autonomous agents using natural language and frozen LLMs, enhancing their ability to generalize and adapt across various tasks.
  • DRDO Internship: Explored fine-tuning strategies for large language models, focusing on optimization methods in NLP tasks.
  • Ulster University RAG Application: Led the development of a Retrieval-Augmented Generation system, integrating advanced chatbots with user-uploaded content.
